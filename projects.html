Air Math Calculator

What is it?

t’s a real-time computer vision-based tool that turns a webcam into a virtual whiteboard, allowing users to draw math expressions in the air using only their index finger. The system uses fingertip tracking powered by MediaPipe to trace gestures onto a digital canvas.


What Does it Do?

It continuously monitors the webcam feed, detects when the user’s index finger is extended, and draws its movement onto a white canvas. Once the user finishes drawing, the tool uses OCR to convert the air-drawn expression into text and evaluates the result instantly.


How Does it Help?

It enables a hands-free and hardware-free method for solving math, ideal for presentations, classroom use, or individuals with mobility limitations. By combining gesture recognition with math OCR, it makes learning and computation more interactive, accessible, and engaging.
