My Business Portfolio and Case Competitions

BDO Mind Munchies

Mind Munchies is a concept created for the BDO New Venture case competition, proposing natural cookies aimed at enhancing concentration and focus for university students. The idea included snacks with brain-boosting ingredients like Theobromine, Creatine, and Omega-3, providing a healthier alternative to caffeinated drinks. I showcased my finance, marketing, and leadership skills by guiding and contributing to this innovative solution.

Wealthsimple Learn

I proposed the WealthSimple Learn app concept during the WealthSimple Case Competition, blending financial insight with marketing strategy. Leading a diverse team, I demonstrated leadership and analytical skills, designing a customer-focused app to enhance financial literacy among students. This project highlighted my ability to integrate educational content with practical tools.

Rize Sport Event Management Company

At RIZE, a sports event management company, I was deeply involved in both outreach and financial operations. I financed and secured venues, engaged sponsors, and managed financial tasks such as maintaining balance sheets, providing a solid foundation for financial health and precise event cost analysis. This role strengthened my financial management and communication skills in a dynamic context.
